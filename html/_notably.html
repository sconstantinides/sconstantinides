Notably

UX, UI, Back-end, Front-end

Notably is a newsletter of important updates from your friends. Users can choose to receive daily or weekly email updates. Notably was built using the Facebook API.

The problem

Friends move, switch jobs, and fall in (and out of) love often. Outside of your close friends, there's no easy way to keep track of what's going on.

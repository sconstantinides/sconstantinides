Notably

UX, UI, Back-end, Front-end

Notably is a newsletter of important updates from your friends. Users receive a daily or weekly email update. I built Notably to solve a my own problem using the Facebook API.

The problem

Friends move, switch jobs, and fall in (and out of) love often. Outside of your close friends, there’s no easy way to keep track of what’s going on.

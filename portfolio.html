Reliefwatch

UX, UI, Back-end, Front-end

Reliefwatch is supply chain software for health organizations in the developing world.

The problem

Conventional supply chain solutions rely on infrastructure that doesn't yet exist. However, most people have access to basic mobile phones.

The solution

Reliefwatch uses voice calls to gather data from remote health centers. As CPO, I helped design and build the responsive web interface.
